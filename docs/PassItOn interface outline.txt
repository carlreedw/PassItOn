PassItOn interface:
	
LOW LEVEL
	## These functions are NOT unit testable -- they only get data from database
	getUser
		needs (database)
		makes $this->user
	getDAGs
		needs (database)
		makes $this->dags
	getProjectIDs
		needs (database)
		makes $this->project_ids
	getEventIDs
		needs (database)
		makes $this->event_ids
	getEDCData
		needs (database)
		makes $this->edc_data
	getScreeningData
		needs (database)
		makes $this->screening_data
	getUADData
		needs (database)
		makes $this->uad_data
	
MID LEVEL
	## This function depends on lower level functions but requires database access too -- minimize business logic allowed
		
HIGH LEVEL
	## The following functions ARE unit testable (after supplying instance with needed data)
	getRecords
		needs user, dags, project_ids, event_ids, edc_data, screening_data, uad_data
		makes $this->records
	authorizeUser
		needs user
		makes $this->user->authorized (t/f)
	getMySiteData
		needs records
		makes $this->my_site_data
	getAllSitesData
		needs records
		makes $this->all_sites_data
	showMySite
		needs my_site_data
		makes (html to send to user's browser)
	showAllSites
		needs all_sites_data
		makes (html to send to user's browser)
